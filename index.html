Coagula

Novel treatment for blood disorders Hemophilia A and severe cases of Vonwillebrand Disease.

Hemophilia and von Willebrand disease are most common bleeding disorders affecting humans. A bleeding disorder is a genetic blood clotting deficiency caused by the absence or inactivity of an essential blood clotting protein, or “factor”. This deficiency causes the body to form unstable blood clots that allow bleeding to continue longer than normal. A person with a bleeding disorder can experience frequent and often spontaneous episodes of prolonged bleeding. The most severe cases can have extensive bleeding into joints, muscles and vital organs, resulting in life-threatening emergencies. Hemophilia and von Willebrand disease are genetically inherited. Both require intrusive, repeated medical procedures.


Coagula will improve the lives of Hemophilia A and von Willebrand Disease patients. It will lessen customer pain through the convenience of fewer injections. The treatment travels throughout the entire body andhas much longer lifespan in the body than other similar drugs. Finally, Coagula can be created synthetically, which will ensure sterilization and significantly reduce the chance of infection in the patient.

VitaClick

An updated, cost effective delivery mechanism for epinephrine.

Vitaclick is our company’s solution to making life with allergies better.


Vitaclick is a more cost efficient and user friendly alternative to self injection epinephrine pens. Currently, all auto injection epinephrine pens are one use, bulky, and expensive.Vita click is reusable, smaller, and less costly. The design of the pen is much more compact, and keeps the size minimal and convenient. The pen is also designed to use cartridges that contain the needle and epinephrine within, so the pen becomes reusable (no additional purchases are necessary). Lastly, because the user only has to buy one auto injector pen, the expense becomes fractional. Thus, the cartridges allow the user to purchase just one pen and easily carry cartridges with them without taking up much space. All a user must do is place the cartridge in, position the pen, press a button, and click!

MeshCare

A refined surgical mesh that reduces adhesion formation and free radical production.

MeshCare is a coated surgical mesh product that aims to reduce post-operative adhesion scarring in the bowels of patients who have undergone a hernia repair surgery, specifically inguinal hernioplasty. MeshCare is mainly targeted towards males, who have an increased risk for inguinal hernias in the inguinal canals. Resulting adhesions post-operation may result in bowel strangulation, tissue death, and gangrene. The technology behind this product utilizes inhibitory molecules against transforming growth factor-beta (TGF-β) in order to decrease collagen deposition, and thus scarring. These inhibitory molecules prevent synthesis of the TGF-β on a certain localized area of the mesh (and consequently a localized area of the body) thereby directing scar formation and limiting excess scar tissue growth onto the bowels. MeshCare is implanted into the body in normal inguinal hernioplasties, and delivers inhibitor molecules via release upon contact with cells in the abdominal wall.
